Codfish With Cream:

Codfish with cream, or Bacalhau com Natas, is a beloved Portuguese dish that combines flaky, salted codfish with a rich , creamy sauce. Typically, the cod is cooked with onions, garlic, and sometimes potatoes, then layered with a velvety mixture of cream and bechamel sauce. It's baked until golden and bubbling, offering a comforting, savory meal that showcases the best of Portuguese cuisine. The dish is often enjoyed as a hearty family meal, especially during festive occasions.

Caldo Verde:

Caldo Verde is a traditional Portuguese soup that's both comforting and flavorful. Made with simple ingredients like kale (or collard greens) , potatoes, onions, and garlic, it's often enriched with chorizo or linguica sausage for added depth. The soup is known for its vibrant green color, which comes from finely chopped greens. It's a popular dish in Portugal, particularly during festivals and family gatherings , offering a deliciously warm and healthy meal that's perfect for cooler weather.

Broa:

Broa is a traditional Portuguese cornbread with a dense texture and slightly crunchy crust. Made from cornmeal mixed with wheats or rye flour, it has rustic flavor and is often enjoyed with soups like Caldo Verde or as a side to meats and cheeses.
